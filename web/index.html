Introduction

We are thrilled to introduce Black Rocket OS and Black Rocket Phone, the ultimate secure phone and operating system that guarantees unparalleled privacy and security. In an era where data breaches and privacy concerns are rampant, we aim to provide a solution that puts you back in control of your digital life.

Black Rocket OS is a fork of Graphene OS and it is built on its robust foundation, renowned for its security and privacy features. We've taken it a step further to deliver the best experience in secure mobile communication.

Who We Are

We are a team of passionate tech enthusiasts, developers, and privacy advocates from all over the world, united by our commitment to creating a safer digital world. Our mission is to provide an accessible and user-friendly solution to everyone who values their privacy and security.

With years of experience in software development and cybersecurity, we have come together under the jurisdiction of Liechtenstein, through the company IntelliSoftAlpin, to create Black Rocket OS — a project that reflects our dedication and expertise.

The Problem

In today’s digital age, our smartphones are treasure troves of personal information. Unfortunately, mainstream operating systems and devices are often plagued with vulnerabilities, leaving your data exposed to hackers, surveillance, and corporate snooping.

Privacy should not be a luxury—it should be a standard.

This is why we are developing Black Rocket OS, to give you the freedom and peace of mind you deserve.
